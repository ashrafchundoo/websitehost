About US

The serie of conferences aims to create a networking opportunity for both researchers and practitioners to discuss recent insights on socially responsible practices in the non- and for-profit sector. We welcome scholarly papers and practice contributions on the following topics: CSR and Sustainability, CSR and Business Ethics, CSR, Social Media and Grass-Roots Campaigns, CSR and Education, Communicating CSR, CSR Initiatives and Strategies, Corporate Governance, NGO Marketing, CSR in the Digital Age, CSR, Reputation and Crisis Management, Ethical Stakeholder Engagement.
